All you need to do is to type in your location into the search bar and and it will show all of the gyms near your location. GYMtrition

An interactive website that allows you to find gyms, create a workout list, and search for nutritional facts of different food items. GYMtrition was created and designed as a front-end project by students enrolled in Rutgers Coding Bootcamp. The langauges, frameworks, and API's used for to create this website were HTML, CSS, Javascript, jQuery, MaterializeCSS, AJAX, Google Maps, and USDA.

Create Your Own Workout Routine

Here in GYMtrition we want to provide a service that allows you to find exercises and develop your own workout plan. Our Workout Builder is designed for you to look at different exercises for each muscle group. Each exercise provides a description of that workout, the equipment you will need, as well as a video of how to properly perform the exercise. We also have a feature that allows you add any of the exercises to a sturctured list. In the Workout List feature you can develop your own workout plan and add any workout to the list. The list also allows you to put in the amount of reps, sets, and weight that you would like to complete for a particular exercise. Click the button below to get started!

GYMtrition

An interactive website that allows you to find gyms, create a workout list, and search for nutritional facts of different food items. GYMtrition was created and designed as a front-end project by students enrolled in Rutgers Coding Bootcamp. The languages, frameworks, and API's used for to create this website were HTML, CSS, Javascript, jQuery, MaterializeCSS, AJAX, Google Maps, and USDA.
